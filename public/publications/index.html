GenLARP: Enabling Immersive Live Action Role-Play through LLM-Generated Worlds and Characters

GenLARP is an AI-drive Live Action Role-Play (LARP) system that generates interactive 3D environments and characters in real time using LLMs, supporting immersive narrative role play for education and experiential learning.

September 2025 · Yichen Yu*, Yifan Jiang*, Mandy Lui, Qiao Jin

EmbodiedCreate: In-Situ 3D Authoring for Analogical Learning

EmbodiedCreate is a system that leverages LLMs and UnityMCP to empower students to create 3D analogies and gain a better understanding of AI recommendation systems. In this playground, students can experience a baseline VR analogy (BeeTrap), experience the modified analogies, and create their own analogies.

September 2025 · Shutong Wu, Hecong Wang, Yifan Jiang, Xiaofei Zhou, Jiaqi Lu, Zhen Bai
