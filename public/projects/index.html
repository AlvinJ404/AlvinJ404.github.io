JengaX

JengaX is a VR system that transforms a traditional physics classroom into an interactive playground. Students can access and interact with 3D-rendered objects by simply touching or dragging a 2D figure in the classroom presentation. It is in its early development stage and we are conducting literature review.

September 2025 · Yichen Yu*, Yifan Jiang*, Qiao Jin

GenLARP: Enabling Immersive Live Action Role-Play through LLM-Generated Worlds and Characters

GenLARP is an AI-drive Live Action Role-Play (LARP) system that generates interactive 3D environments and characters in real time using LLMs, supporting immersive narrative role play for education and experiential learning. We are currently extending this research project into a full conference paper.

September 2025 · Yichen Yu*, Yifan Jiang*, Mandy Lui, Qiao Jin
